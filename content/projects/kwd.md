overview: >-
  The Kirkwood is a 26 unit value-add property located in Detroit's New Center
  neighborhood, immediately north of Henry Ford Hospital's main campus. The
  property was acquired in 2021 with significant deferred maintenance and
  below-market rents. The value-add plan included the renovation of all 26
  units as well as interior and exterior common area improvements. This
  workforce housing asset benefits from the City of Detroit PILOT program,
  significantly reducing property tax expenses while maintaining affordable
  rents at or below 80% AMI.
